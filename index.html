About

I’m a student and 3D animator who enjoys the craft of motion. I work mainly in Maya doing mocap cleanup, manual animation, and hand-polishing work. I’ve studied the 12 principles of animation and enjoy tackling tricky timing and weight challenges. I prefer a simple workflow: find the story in the motion, then refine until it feels right. This portfolio is a small collection of work and thoughts — made by me, for people who love animation.
